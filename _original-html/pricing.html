Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes — upgrade instantly, downgrade at cycle end. Either way you always keep the value of what you paid.

Free and Starter plans show a small "Made with InvoicePro" tag on the PDF. Pro removes it completely — your invoice, your brand, nothing else.

30-day full refund on all paid plans. No questions, no friction. Just contact support within 30 days and your money is back.
